Super-resolution fight club: assessment of 2D and 3D single-molecule localization microscopy software

This paper compares state-of-the-art algorithms in single molecule localisation microscopy (SMLM). The alternating descent conditional gradient (ADCG) method along with other variants, which all fall into the class of algorithms derived from off-the-grid compressed sensing, consistently gave the best results for 2D high spot density.

The paper does not analyse deep learning based approaches such as DeepLoco, but says that they show great promise especially for modelling more complex forward models.
